Terrain Shooter

W/S move. A/D or ←/→ turn movement. Q/E strafe. Left-drag: movement direction. Right-drag: camera. Both mouse buttons: back. 1-5: action bar. Click door to open.

Inventory

Click an inventory block to highlight it, then click a slot to assign. Press 1-5 to place that slot's block. Right-click to place too.
